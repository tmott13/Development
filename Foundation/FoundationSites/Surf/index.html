A store for the new or experienced surfer

If you need lessons, we have some of the best instructors in Hawaii ready to teach you. We can offer surf lessons, bodyboarding lessons and stand up paddle lessons for all skills.

Or maybe you need a board for a day. Our boards are available for purchase or for rent for 3, 6, and 12 hour blocks of time.
